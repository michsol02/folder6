Memberships With Responsible 100 

Responsible 100 is a community of people from a variety of backgrounds who represent the widest range of organisations. What unites us is our desire to use business to improve society and protect the environment. If you seek to invest your time and energy in ways that can truly enable you to reduce your negative impacts on people and planet, and increase your positive impacts, to your fullest potential, then please join us.

SHARE YOUR EXPERIENCES & STORIES

Showcase what you do well, what is work in progress, and also what you struggle with (no business can ever be perfect after all)

EXPLORE BETTER PRACTICES

Benchmark how you currently perform as POOR, OKAY, GOOD or EXCELLENT

GET MATCHED UP

Learn from expert partners from a multitude of backgrounds and collaborate with other businesses developing better practices

CONTRIBUTE TO OUR BENCHMARKS

As more members share more examples of what they do, our benchmarks become ever-more informed and more valuable to assess against

Memberships Include

Showcasing
Being assessed against an ever growing set of benchmarks. 

Sharing your stories with relevance, value and credibility.

A listing in our directory and access to our badge to communicate your values to your stakeholders.
Consulting On Demand
 The Responsible 100 team are all highly informed on the wide range of responsibility issues we explore. Where our expertise is lacking, we reach out to expert partners and friends. The help you need, when you need it, is always at hand.
Develop Your Business With Other Members
Access to the Responsible 100 LinkedIn and WhatsApp channels to participate in discussions and reach out to fellow members directly.

Access to the benchmarks and the related resources to tap into the knowledge of the community at a time that suits you.

Contribute To Other Good Businesses
We match together those businesses that we know will be able to support one another given shared challenges and experiences or other complementary criteria.

You make valuable contributions every time you share your experiences of challenges that you have faced, how they impacted your business, what you did and why, and what the results, and subsequent course corrections were.

We’re alway exploring new ways to enable participants to share and connect with other businesses that are also on this journey, whether matched 1:2:1 or in a group.

Join an accountability group where your critical friends will provide feedback with a positive, supportive mindset.
Promotion And Marketing, When The Time Suits You
Receive shout outs on our social media channels and to our mailing list to increase your credibility and reach.

KEY PARTICIPATION TERMS

All the Responsible 100 benchmarks and all the knowledge contained therein, in addition to most workshops delivered by partner organisations, are provided to participants as part of a monthly subscription fee package.   

Participation requires minimum time investments from the participant business or organisation. Costs are based on the amount of time Responsible 100's team and partners dedicate to the participant. 

Rolling over hours: unused hours in any given month can be rolled over to the next month but cannot be rolled over to any subsequent months.
